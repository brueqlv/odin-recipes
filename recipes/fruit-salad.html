Fruit Salad

Description

This fruit salad is perfect for a backyard bbq or any occasion. There are never leftovers! This is one of my favorite fruit salad recipes, as I think the citrusy sauce really makes it. This salad is tastier the longer you can let it soak in its juices. I prefer 3 to 4 hours in the refrigerator before I serve it.

Ingredients

  • fresh orange
  • lemon
  • brown sugar
  • grated orange zests
  • grated lemon zests
  • vanilla extract
  • pineapple
  • strawberries,
  • kiwis
  • bananas
  • oranges
  • grapes
  • blueberries

Steps

  1. Make the souce on the stove and let it cool.
  2. Arrange the fruits in a container, then pur the souce over them.
  3. Cover and refrigerate to allow the flawors to meld.
